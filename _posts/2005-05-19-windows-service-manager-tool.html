--- layout: post title: "Windows Service Manager Tool" date: 2005-05-19 comments: false ---
If you have to start/stop services at all during a typically development day then this free tool will probably be a great help to you.

It lets you create simple profiles that start or stop various services. So if your running CF 6 and CF 7 on the same box you can jump between the two profiles very easilly. Check it out!

In the future they are talking about letting you potentially queue the order of the start/stop requests which would be really handy!
