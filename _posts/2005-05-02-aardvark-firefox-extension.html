The other day I was over at del.icio.us and saw a bunch of new bookmarks to Aardvark, a firefox extension, but I ignored them. The today, at fullasagoog, I saw a link to digitalmediaminute that was also talking about Aardvark so I decided I could at least check it out.

It's very very cool; at least from a web developers perspective. It gives you another nice tool for debugging CSS.

If you haven't seen it yet, or even heard of it, consider this your invitation to go check it out. You will probably be glad you did.
